How it works

Up and running in 3 minutes

No technical setup. No browser extensions. Just paste URLs and we handle everything.

01

Add URLs to monitor

Paste any public URL — a vendor's terms of service, a regulatory page, a competitor's pricing page. Set how often you want us to check it (hourly, daily, weekly).

02

We watch it around the clock

Our system checks every URL on your schedule. We store a full snapshot each time so we can compare versions precisely.

03

Get alerted the moment anything changes

The instant we detect a change, we send you an alert with the exact diff — what was removed (in red) and what was added (in green). No noise, just signal.

04

Review, annotate, and share

Open the change in your dashboard, leave notes for your team, and export the diff for your audit trail. Full history kept forever.
